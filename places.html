To See:

The viewing gallery (The View from The Shard) offers the most breathtaking view of London. During daytime, London's sceneries are best viewed from the grond level. Therefore, the best time to visit The Shard is at night. You can also enjoy a glass of cocktail from the bar when appreciating the nightview of London.
